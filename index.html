Privacy Policy

Welcome to Sleepy Sounds! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information collected automatically through the app and third-party SDKs:

  • User-Provided Information: Information you submit, such as feedback, support requests, or bug reports. This may include your email address or other details you choose to provide.
  • Device Information: Device name, manufacturer, OS version, and other diagnostic information for bug reporting and app functionality.
  • In-App Purchase Information: Subscription and purchase details are collected via RevenueCat to manage access and entitlements.
  • Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics collects anonymized usage data to help us improve app performance and user experience.

Third-Party SDKs

We use third-party services to help provide and improve our app:

  • RevenueCat: Collects purchase and device information to manage subscriptions. Data is linked to the user/device but not used for advertising or cross-app tracking.
  • Firebase Analytics: Collects anonymized analytics data linked to device identifiers to improve app functionality. Data is not used for advertising or tracking users across apps.

Use of Information

Information collected is used to:

  • Manage subscriptions and entitlements
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Respond to support inquiries and feedback
